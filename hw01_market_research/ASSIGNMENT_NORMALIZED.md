# Assignment 1: Competitive Analysis and Reference Research

### Objective
To evaluate the competitive landscape for your product and compare the effectiveness of traditional research methods against AI-assisted workflows (using varying levels of prompt engineering).

---

### Task Description

#### Step 1: Case Selection
*   State the product name and provide a brief summary (1–2 sentences).
*   *Note: You may choose a project from the list below or use your own.*

#### Step 2: Conduct a Classic Competitive Analysis
*   Perform manual research to identify and study 3–4 direct or indirect competitors/references.
*   **Deliverable:** A summary table.
    *   **Columns:** Name, link, key feature (USP), what it lacks + your specific comparison criteria.
    *   **Comparison Criteria:** You must justify why you chose these specific parameters (e.g., pricing, UX, mobile app availability, loyalty program).

#### Step 3: Simple AI Prompt
*   Submit a free-form query to any AI (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.).
*   **Deliverables:**
    *   A screenshot or the text of your prompt.
    *   The AI’s response.
    *   **Internal Reflection:** How much of the AI's response is factual, and how much is "hallucination" (made-up information)?

#### Step 4: "COMPOSER" Prompt (Structured Engineering)
*   Use a complex, structured prompt following the framework: **Role + Context + Task + Output Format**.
*   **Deliverables:**
    *   A screenshot or the text of your structured prompt.
    *   The AI’s response.

#### Step 5: Final Conclusion
Compare the three methods (manual, simple AI, structured AI) based on:
*   **Speed:** Which method saved you the most time?
*   **Depth:** Which method revealed non-obvious solutions or deeper insights?
*   **Reliability:** Which method produced the most errors or inaccuracies?
*   **Personal Verdict:** Which method would you choose for a real-world professional project?

---

### Deliverables
Submit your work as a **PDF report** containing all 5 steps, including:
*   Links to the references found in Step 2.
*   Links to the AI conversations (prompts and answers) for Steps 3 and 4.
*   Personal insights and conclusions for Steps 2 and 5.
